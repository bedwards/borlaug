About the Style

Historical fiction in the tradition of Karl Ove Knausgaard's autobiographical novels. The story emerges from the mundane. The language illuminates the mundane. The themes emerge from the mundane. A man in a field, bent over wheat plants, year after year. The heat. The stool. The plastic. The specimens. The tweezers. The hours. The years.
